          Welcome to Tic-Tac-toe.

          Instructions:

            1) You will be prompted to choose your marker X or O.

            2) You can choose to play agains the computer or against
               another player.

            3) The game is played on a a grid that's 3 square
               by 3 squares

            4)Enter the board position to select your marker:

                        7 | 8 | 9
                        ---------
                        4 | 5 | 6
                        ---------
                        1 | 2 | 3

            5) The winner is the first to get three of his/her mark
               in a row (up, down, accross or diagonally)

            6) The game is over is all 9 squares are full

            Enjoy the game !
